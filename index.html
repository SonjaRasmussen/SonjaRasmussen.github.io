About Me

I am a Canadian living in Seattle. I moved to California after my first son was born in 2012 and then to Seattle in 2018. My career as an accountant in Financial Services lasted 10 years, and I had many adventures. I Lived in Winnipeg Manitoba for one really cold winter to work for an Insurance Company, then moved to Bermuda, to work in the Hedge Fund industry and finally, found my way to Calgary Alberta to work at an energy company on a trading floor .

I am a mother of two very active young boys. In 2018 I began a career change and enrolled in the University of Washington Coding Boot Camp. I am enjoying the transition to coding and seeing so much opportunity for women in tech. I love having the skills to build applications that solve every day problems.
